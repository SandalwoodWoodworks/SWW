The Art of Bespoke

At Sandalwood Woodworks, we believe that furniture shouldn’t just occupy space—it should define it. Led by a commitment to true heirloom quality, every piece we design tells a story of patience, architectural precision, and deep respect for our materials.

We work closely with clients, interior designers, and architects to manifest completely custom commissions. From selecting unique, sustainable logs to laying the final micro-fine hand rub, our process ensures your vision lasts generations.
